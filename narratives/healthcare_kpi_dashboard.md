**Healthcare KPI Reporting Dashboard (Power BI)**

Developed a reporting dashboard that translated clinical and operational data into accessible visual insights for leadership. Highlighted trends, patient flow metrics, and operational bottlenecks in a clear, communication friendly format.

**Project Overview**

This project highlights my ability to transform clinical and operational data into clear, accessible insights that support decision making for healthcare leadership.

**Objective**

To develop a dashboard that visualizes key performance indicators (KPIs) related to patient flow, appointment volume, wait times, and operational efficiency.

**Approach**

-	Cleaned and validated raw clinical and operational data.
-	Designed a Power BI dashboard with intuitive navigation and clear visual hierarchy.
-	Created DAX measures to track trends, bottlenecks, and performance changes over time.
-	Structured the dashboard to be easily understood by non technical clinical leaders.

**Outcome**

-	Provided leadership with a clear view of operational trends.
-	Improved communication around performance metrics.
-	Enabled data supported decision making for staffing, scheduling, and workflow improvements.

Skills Demonstrated
Data storytelling • KPI reporting • Dashboard design • Healthcare operations • Communication through visuals
